Symbolism

Ox (牛)

Within Chinese culture, the ox often symbolizes weath, diligence, and perseverance.

In the Chinese zodiac, the ox is second within the list of twelve, as it is said that the ox came in second place within the great race of the twelve zodiacal creatures.

Magpie (喜鹊)

Within Chinese culture, the magpie often symbolizes happiness and good luck.

The initial Chinese character for magpie (喜) means "happiness" and the magpie was once known as the bird of joy.
